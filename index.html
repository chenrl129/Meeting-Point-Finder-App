Welcome to the Meeting Point Finder!

Here’s how to find the perfect meeting spot in 3 easy steps:

1. Add Locations

Click on the map or search for an address to add locations for you and your friends.

2. Calculate

Click "Find Center Point" for the geographic middle or "Estimate Travel Times" for time-based suggestions.
